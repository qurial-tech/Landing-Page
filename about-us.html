Our Story

Our journey as a company began with two tech-savvy founders who were driven by a common vision: to make healthcare more accessible and affordable for everyone. Despite their technical expertise, they found themselves frustrated with the complexities of navigating the healthcare system, especially when it came to managing chronic diseases. That's why they decided to do something about it

Inspired by their own experiences, they brought on medical experts to the team and began developing a patient engagement platform that would revolutionize the way people manage their health. The result is a smart AI-powered assistant that helps patients with chronic diseases like coronary artery disease (CAD) stay on top of their care journey, from lifestyle changes to reminders for doctor appointments.

Our platform leverages the power of WhatsApp, which has over 500 million users in India, and supports multilingual capabilities so that patients can receive information in their preferred language. By integrating with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, we are poised to make a meaningful impact on the lives of millions of people across India.

We are dedicated to empowering patients with the tools and support they need to take control of their health. Join us on our mission to create a world where healthcare is accessible and affordable for all.
